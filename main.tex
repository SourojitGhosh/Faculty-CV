\section{Doctoral Thesis}

\noindent\textbf{\large{Exploring Representational Harms of Generative AI Systems upon \\  Historically  Marginalized Populations often Overlooked in Fairness Research}}\hfill\textcolor{uwpurple}{\textit{June 2026}}\par

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=2pt, topsep=4pt]
  \item GAI tools replicate harmful stereotypes, consistently overlooking vast sections of \\ historically marginalized populations in fairness research, exposing critical gaps in the field.
  \item To demonstrate this, I present three case studies examining gender bias in low-resource language translation, Western stereotyping of Indian culture in text-to-image tools, and ``castelessness" in my study of AI representations of caste-oppressed communities.
  \item Together, these findings lead me to motivate a broader, more expansive research agenda toward inclusive AI fairness practices and meaningful social good for overlooked communities worldwide.
\end{itemize}
